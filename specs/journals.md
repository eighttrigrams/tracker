# Feature: Journals

Journal Entries are shown on the Resources page (in contrast to Resources, though, they don't have an url field associated with them, or shown to the user).
Like there are Tasks that are grouped as Recurrent Tasks or Meets which are parts of Meeting Series, Journal Entries are grouped as Journals. When I select "Journal mode"
then I can create new Journals. An initial modal shows me whether that should be a Weekly or a Daily Journal (which our worker creates either Weekly or Daily entries for, 
at the start of the week or at the start of the day).

## Journal Entries on the Today page.

Right in the row of the Days, where on the right hand side is the Day picker group, on the left hand side, left aligned in that space, is a button called "Journals". Upon
entering Journals mode, all other sections, like Urgent Matters or Upcoming and the Day section disapear and we are left with currently active, for today or this week, Journal Entries.
Only other thing that stays is the Overdue section.

## Resources page behaviour

On the Resources page, when I change into Journals mode, I see the Journals, and then can, as usual, filter ***by** them, such that I only see Journal Entries of that particular Journal.
In that mode, the Importance and "Manual/Added" sort order options disappear. Journals Entries are always listed in the sort order most recently created first. Also, the add/search bar, disappears.
