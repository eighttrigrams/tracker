# Feature: Ledgers

Ledgers are blank 'sheets' for tabs you keep on things, ongoing records, backlogs, notes and lists.
They appear in the Resources page, and where it normally shows an url left to the title, to filter by,
ledgers show "Ledger", and I can normally filter by them, as I can filter by domains.

They behave very much like Resources, but don't have an URL associated with them. That means that our
combined search/add bar now accepts either urls, or just some string, and depending on what it is,
either a Resource or a Ledger are being created.
